about

journey.md

I'm a platform engineer working at the seam between customer data infrastructure and the campaigns that run on top of it. Most of my time goes to Adobe Experience Platform — schemas, identity resolution, ingestion pipelines — and to making that data actually usable downstream.

I hold a Master's in Computer Application and work across JavaScript, Node.js, React and Python. The thread through all of it is the same: take messy, fragmented data and turn it into something a marketing team can act on the same day.

Lately that's meant applied GenAI — shipping an LLM product to production, including the unglamorous parts like token budgeting, rate limiting and caching that decide whether it survives contact with real traffic.
